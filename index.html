ABOUT ME

Dr. Abdelrahim Salih is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Geography at King Faisal University. He is a dedicated professional with 15+ work experience in Remote Sensing, Environmental Sciences, GIS, and machine learning. He is specializing in mapping, monitoring and assessing natural resources and environmental changes, dealing with spatial data manipulation, analysing and modeming of geo-spatial data. He earned an adequate knowledge of LiDAR data processing and features detection.
